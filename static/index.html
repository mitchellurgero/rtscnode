How This Works

RTSC is built around the idea of "Round Tables" (Hence the name!). This means that everyone comes together and can vote on different things and talk about them as well.

This means that everyone can vote on who can join, who is kicked from the room, etc.

RTSC also uses OpenPGP Javascript libraries to encrypt all chat communications. However - voting is NOT encrypted this way because no PGP private keys are sent to the server. Voting is however sent over encrypted SSL tunnles to ensure the highest available safety for you is ensured.
